Reading **City Limits edited by Tikender Panwar**.

Things around me, which I used to think of as sustainable development, have actually given rise to serious issues like gentrification, the commoditisation of basic amenities like water, electricity, and housing, and increased unemployment. There was a time when a government job could provide enough financial security to build a house and educate a family. Today, it is difficult for me to imagine being able to achieve the same. I am seriously questioning whether this is "development" in the first place.
